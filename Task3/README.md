# 🛡️ Task 3: Cyber Security Awareness

## 📌 Overview
This task focuses on understanding common cyber threats and promoting awareness of safe online practices. The goal is to help users recognize risks and protect themselves in the digital environment.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand cybersecurity fundamentals  
- Identify common threats  
- Learn best practices for online safety  
- Promote awareness and prevention  

---

## 🔍 Topics Covered

### 🎣 Phishing
Attackers attempt to trick users into revealing sensitive information through fake emails or websites.

### 🦠 Malware
Malicious software that damages systems or steals data.

### 🔒 Ransomware
Blocks access to data until a ransom is paid.

### 🧠 Social Engineering
Manipulating users into giving confidential information.

---

## 🔑 Password Security
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Avoid personal information  
- Do not reuse passwords  

---

## 🔐 Two-Factor Authentication
Adds an extra layer of protection beyond passwords.

---

## 🌐 Safe Browsing
- Avoid suspicious websites  
- Check for HTTPS  
- Keep software updated  

---

## 📧 Email Safety
- Verify sender identity  
- Avoid clicking unknown links  
- Be cautious with attachments  

---

## ✔ Best Practices
- Enable 2FA  
- Use strong passwords  
- Stay updated on threats
